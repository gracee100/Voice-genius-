How to Use VoiceGenius

What is TTS and STT?

Text-to-Speech (TTS): A technology that converts written text into spoken words. It is useful for listening to written content, language learning, and accessibility.

Speech-to-Text (STT): A technology that converts spoken words into written text. It is useful for transcription, voice commands, and communication.

How to Use the TTS Feature

  1. Enter your text in the input box.
  2. Select a language and voice from the dropdown menus.
  3. Adjust the speech rate, pitch, and volume using the sliders.
  4. Choose an emotion to modify how the text sounds.
  5. Click the "Play" button to hear the text spoken aloud.
  6. Use the "Pause," "Resume," and "Stop" buttons to control playback.
  7. Click the "Download" button to save the speech as an audio file.
  8. Use the translation feature to convert text to another language before speaking.

How to Use the STT Feature

  1. Click the "Start Recording" button to begin capturing your speech.
  2. Speak clearly into your microphone.
  3. Click the "Stop Recording" button to end the session.
  4. View the transcribed text in the output box.
  5. Click the "Copy" button to copy the text to your clipboard.
  6. Enable real-time translation to get your speech translated as you speak.

How to Use Interactive Storytelling

  1. Select a story from the dropdown menu.
  2. Click "Start Story" to begin the narration.
  3. The story will pause at key moments to ask you questions.
  4. Choose an option or speak your answer to continue the story.
  5. Use the controls to pause, continue, or stop the story.

Tips for Best Results

  • Speak clearly and at a moderate pace for accurate STT transcription.
  • Use a high-quality microphone for better audio input.
  • Experiment with different voices and settings in TTS to find your preferred style.
  • Try different emotions to see how they affect the speech output.
  • For translation, use simple and clear sentences for best results.
  • In a noisy environment, use an external microphone for STT.

FAQs

Can I use this app offline?

No, the app requires an internet connection to access TTS and STT APIs.

What languages are supported?

The app supports multiple languages for both TTS and STT. Check the dropdown menus for available options.

Is my data saved?

No, your data is not stored on our servers. All processing happens in real-time.

Why can't the app access my microphone?

Make sure you've granted microphone permissions in your browser settings. Also check that no other application is using the microphone.

Can I save my custom voice settings?

Yes, use the "Save Custom Voice" button to store your voice settings for future sessions.
